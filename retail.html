Bronze Boost Tanning Water 2oz

$15.00

A clear self-tanning mist designed to refresh and deepen your color between spray tans.

  • Refresh color between spray tans
  • Adds a subtle boost of color without streaking
  • Clear formula with zero risk of transfer to clothes or sheets
  • Perfect for maintaining your glow between appointments

DirectionsApply 4-8 sprays from an arms-length distance onto desired areas. Use sparingly on knees, elbows, and ankles. Rinse in 8-12 hours with lukewarm water only - no soaps or scrubs. Moisturize daily to maintain skin hydration for long-lasting color.

Key ingredientsVitamin C, Cucumber Extract, Aloe Vera, Bamboo Extract, Jojoba Seed Oil, White Tea Extract

Bronze Glow Tan Extender 2oz

$10.00

A nourishing tan-extending lotion designed to maintain hydration and help your spray tan fade more evenly.

  • Extend your tan up to several extra days
  • Hydrates skin for more even fading
  • Gradually maintains color for a longer-lasting tan
  • Helps your spray tan fade evenly instead of patchy

DirectionsApply once per day, every other day, starting after day three of spray tan application to extend the life of your spray tan. Wash hands immediately after application to prevent staining.

Key ingredientsSoybean Oil, Hyaluronic Acid, Kakadu Plum Fruit Extract, Vitamin C, Aloe, Jojoba Seed Oil, Cucumber Fruit Extract
