Cancellation Policy

Spray Tan Cancellation Policy in Holiday, FL

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, I kindly ask that you provide at least a 24 hours' notice. Failure to do so will result in a cancellation fee of 50% of the service fee being charged.

If you are 15+ minutes late, your appointment will be cancelled and a cancellation fee of 50% of the service fee being charged will incur.

If a mobile appointment is canceled with less than 24 hours' notice, a cancellation fee of 50% of the service fee will be charged. If I am already on the way or have arrived at your location, a 50% service fee, including the travel fee, will be incurred.

This policy allows me to make adjustments to my schedule and accommodate other clients who may be waiting for appointments. Thank you for understanding and for choosing Coastal Bronze Spray Tanning!
